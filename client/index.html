Office Shrub will help prevent the untimely doom of your office plants!

As part of the IoT613 conference in Ottawa, Canada, Macadamian developed a simple IOT solution that will provide up to the minute light and water levels for your office plants. Watch your plants tweet with the press of a button or when you talk to them on Twitter. Follow the default plant on Twitter!
